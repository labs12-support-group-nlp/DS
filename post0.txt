<p>After moving to a new country, my skin began to break out. For half of the year I was looking for the good treatments and doing really painful procedures in order to stop my acne- I succeed a bit. Now I have horrible acne spots and my skin is pigmented. Every morning I run to a mirror to see if some kind of miracle happened to my skin. My mum tries to support me, but every time I cry due to my damaged skin, all she says is - fine, everything will be fine. All my friends have flawless skin and they have no idea of what I might feel. I'm sick of feeling UGLY when i see my reflection without makeup.</p>
